ABOUT US

Foody Eats is driven by the passion to solve issues that matter. It’s the perfect mix of generational farmers and passionate entrepreneurs harnessing expertise in agriculture, environment and supply chain backed by technology to solve issues that matter. Encouraged to positively contribute to the society, our team has studied the pain points of farmers, distributors and consumers. With technological accessibility at a peak we found this to be the perfect time to combine grass root experience and professional learning to find solutions for people beyond our farms and friends. With our experience of managing supply chains for some of the largest companies in the world, we aim to automate and optimize the agri supply chain of Pakistan. This automation enables us to bring the freshest and the cleanest food items to our customer at the lowest price. This optimization reduces waste and brings value to both the producer and the consumer.Our launch has been two years in the making while working with a handful of farmers supplying directly to B2B customers. In 2017, we were successful in empowering our first set of farmers by helping increase crop yields, high value crop allocation and farmer incomes.

Our Vision

We hope to revolutionize the fresh produce supply chain - encouraging farmers to adopt sustainable practices while providing our customers with everyday essentials fresh from the farm.
